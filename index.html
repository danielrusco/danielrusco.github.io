Hi there, I'm Daniel.

Welcome to my website! I'm a graduate student in Seattle, studying counseling psychology. Life is beautiful, terrifying, energizing, sometimes stuck, yet full of potential. I'm fascinated by the question, "what is human flourishing?" I'm pursuing a career as a therapist to help people answer that question.

This site is an opportunity for me to learn a bit about web development. I'm not a developer by trade, I just steal like an artist. Here's to a future of squishing bugs and fixing broken links!

This site is also a place for me to post my writings, curriculum vitae, videos, and visits from the muse, etc.

While you're here, you can read more about who I am and my personal philosophy. I think this stuff is pretty important.
